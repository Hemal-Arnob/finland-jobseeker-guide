Project management roles often require certifications. Commonly recognized certifications in Finland include IPMA (PMA), PMP, and CAPM.

Driving Licence Exchange

International residents can exchange certain foreign driving licenses for Finnish ones. Procedures depend on your original license country.

Check Traficom (Finnish Transport and Communications Agency) for official guidance on exchanging your driving licence.

Official Driving

Health & Safety Courses

Health & safety training is often mandatory, especially in construction, industry, and project management.

Look for certified occupational safety courses through official providers, adult education centers, or company-sponsored programs.
