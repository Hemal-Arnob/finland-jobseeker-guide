  • Apply through multiple websites.

Work Culture & Professional Associations

Finnish work culture values punctuality, trust, independence, and clear communication. Professional associations and unions play an important role.

  • Engineers: Union of Professional Engineers in Finland (UIL / ILRY)
  • Civil Engineering: Finnish Association of Civil Engineers
  • Project Management: IPMA Finland & PMI Finland

Membership improves credibility, access to networking, and continuing education.

Construction Jobs & Requirements

Construction roles may require specific safety cards and certifications.

  • Occupational Safety Card
  • Hot Work Card
  • First Aid (EA1 / EA2)

Some roles related to public safety or structural design may require formal recognition of qualifications.

Project Management in Finland

Project management roles are common in engineering, IT, construction, and energy sectors.

  • IPMA (PMA) Certification
  • PMI – PMP / CAPM

Most employers assess experience directly, but certifications strengthen applications.

Driving Licence Exchange

Foreign driving licences can sometimes be exchanged for a Finnish licence depending on country of origin.

Check requirements via Traficom before applying.

Health & Safety Courses

Mandatory for many industrial, construction, and maintenance roles.

  • Occupational Safety Card
  • Hot Work Safety
  • Electrical Safety (SFS 6002)
