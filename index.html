Search Jobs in Finland

Finding a job in Finland can feel overwhelming at first. Here’s a curated list of trusted job search websites and platforms used in Finland.

LinkedIn

Professional networking platform that also functions as an online CV and job board. Useful for connecting with people in the same (or desired) industry/company, find jobs, and highlight skills/experience not on your CV.

Best Used By: Professionals in white-collar or technical roles (IT/tech, engineering, consulting, business)

Typical Opportunities: Tech, engineering, consulting, and business roles

Notes: Widely used in Finland. Recommended to build a profile and include a link on your CV.

Networking Professional International
Indeed Finland

A platform for job seekers and employers, providing job listings, company reviews, salary info, and resume tools in Finland.

Best Used By: All types of job-seekers

Typical Opportunities: Full-time, part-time, temporary, seasonal, blue-collar, white-collar roles

Aggregator All industries
Työmarkkinatori

National public employment service with job listings, occupation descriptions, and career interviews.

Best Used By: All job-seekers, primarily those registered with TE services

Notes: Creating a profile enables job suggestions, follow-up activities, and messaging from potential employers.

Official Public service
Work in Finland

Government platform offering roles and resources for English-speaking professionals and international talent.

Best Used By: International professionals

Notes: Some jobs may overlap with Työmarkkinatori listings.

Official International
Valtiolle

Official recruitment site for Finnish government and public sector roles.

Best Used By: Public sector career seekers

Notes: Some roles require Finnish/Swedish; hiring processes may be formal and structured.

Government Public sector
Academic Work

Recruitment company connecting early-career professionals, students, and recent graduates.

Best Used By: Students, graduates, early-career professionals

Typical Opportunities: IT, technology, business sectors

Recruitment Early-career
International House Helsinki (IHH)

Non-profit supporting international professionals and families with career assistance, networking, and social programs.

Community Integration Networking
Spouse Program

Supports partners of international professionals seeking employment, career direction, or social activities.

Startup Refugees

Helps refugees and immigrants integrate via employment, skills development, mentoring, and networking.

Herizon

Non-profit career platform connecting skilled international professionals with sustainable careers in Finland and the Nordics.
