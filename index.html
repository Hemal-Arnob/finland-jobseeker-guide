IT & Tech Jobs in Finland

If you are new to Finland and looking for an IT job, many positions are shared on online platforms. These websites are useful for international and immigrant job seekers.

LinkedIn

International job website for professionals. Popular in Finland, especially for IT jobs. Many jobs are in English. Create a profile in English, add skills, education, certifications, work experience, follow Finnish companies, and get endorsements if possible.

International Networking IT-friendly
Jobly

Finnish job website with IT and technology jobs. Jobs can be in English or Finnish. Check language requirements carefully. Useful for beginners and experienced workers.

Private IT
Work in Finland

Official website for international professionals. Offers IT job listings, suitable for English speakers, with information about working and living in Finland.

Official International
Työmarkkinatori

Official Finnish government job website. Free to use, many IT listings, connected to TE Services. Some jobs are available in English. English content is available, and support from employment services can be requested.
