Our website is designed to support all personnel arriving in Finland, as well as highly educated international professionals who are living in Finland. Here, you can find clear, practical, and up-to-date information to help you understand the Finnish job market and navigate your job search with confidence. Whether you are just arriving in Finland or already building your career here, this platform brings together the key resources you need in one place.

Job Search Tips:

  • Many Tech, Engineering, and IT jobs in Finland do not require Finnish, but Finnish language skills are always an advantage.
  • Learning basic Finnish can improve your job opportunities and help you at work and in daily life.
  • Apply through multiple websites to maximize your chances.
  • Keep your CV simple and clear, highlighting your key skills.
  • Ask employment services or integration programs for help when needed.

Finnish Language Learning

Explore a wide range of free, low-cost, and paid Finnish language courses across Finland, both in-person and online. Whether you are a beginner or want to improve your Finnish skills, these courses are structured to help you succeed.

Job Qualification Cards & Certificates

In Finland, many jobs require official certificates or qualification cards showing you have completed training and passed a test for a specific skill or safety requirement. These are often mandatory in sectors like construction, logistics, transport, industry, hospitality, and technical services.
